Cookies & Tracking

Learn how YAA.Group uses cookies and similar technologies and how to manage your preferences.

Use of Cookies

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience, analyze website traffic, and improve site performance. This policy explains what cookies are, what types we use, and how you can manage your preferences.

Types of Cookies

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality and security. These cannot be disabled without affecting site operations.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand user behavior, traffic patterns, and content effectiveness. These collect anonymized data about site usage.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices (language, theme, accessibility options) to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to track campaign effectiveness and deliver relevant content. Optional and require your consent.

How Cookies Work

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember your information and preferences. They do not directly identify individuals but can track browsing patterns.

When you visit our site, we may set cookies to:

  • Remember login information and preferences
  • Measure site performance and traffic patterns
  • Understand how visitors use the site
  • Improve user experience and site functionality
  • Deliver personalized content and recommendations

Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services (analytics, advertising) that set their own cookies. These services operate under their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies to understand how they use your data.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have control over cookies through multiple methods:

  • Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to control cookies through preferences or settings. You can choose to accept, reject, or be notified before cookies are set.
  • Cookie Management: You can delete existing cookies or block specific types (though this may affect site functionality).
  • Do Not Track: Some browsers have "Do Not Track" features that signal your privacy preferences to websites.
  • Site Preferences: You may be able to manage preferences directly on our website through cookie consent banners.

Effects of Disabling Cookies

Disabling cookies may affect your experience on our website. Some features may not work properly, including:

  • Login and authentication services
  • Remembering your preferences
  • Personalized content and recommendations
  • Website analytics and improvements

Consent & Policy Updates

We ask for your consent before setting non-essential cookies. You can withdraw consent at any time through your browser or our website settings. We may update this policy periodically to reflect changes in technology or regulations. Continued use of our site after updates indicates acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about our use of cookies or this policy, please contact us at contact@yaa.group.
